East Coast Ceylon

Description

The East Coast of Sri Lanka is known for its stunning beaches, like Arugam Bay and Trincomalee, as well as cultural sites such as the ancient city of Polonnaruwa. It's a beautiful region with a rich history and diverse wildlife, including opportunities for whale watching and exploring national parks like Kumana and Gal Oya.
